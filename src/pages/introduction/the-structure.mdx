RRPM organises repositories in the following structure

<Code code={`
$PROJECT_ROOT
  |- $DOMAIN
      |- $USER_NAME
          |- $REPO_NAME
`} />

The &nbsp;`$PROJECT_ROOT`&nbsp; can be configured in the [config file](/configuration/config-file).
